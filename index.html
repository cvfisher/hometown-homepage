Visit Shrewsbury

Enjoy culture, history and entertainment in Shropshire's leading market town.

Top three activities to do in Shrewsbury

Visit The Dingle Garden

The Dingle Garden is home to ornamental season flowerbeds, ornamental grass areas, rockeries, shrub bed areas and stunning water features.

Go to the Shrewsbury Museum

Shrewsbury Museum & Art Gallery sits proudly in the town square and is the county's flagship museum and art gallery.

See what's on at the Theatre Severn

The Theatre Severn is situated on Frankwell Quay, a stunning riverside location and one of the main gateways into Shrewsbury.
